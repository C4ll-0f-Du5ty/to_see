Key Features

Ingredient-Based Search

Input any ingredients you have on hand, and Plan_B_Plate will suggest a variety of meals you can make.

Categorized Meals

Search by meal category and explore everything from appetizers to desserts, curated to satisfy your cravings.

Common Meal Search

Simply enter a meal’s name to get a list of recipes, perfect for quick meal prep on busy days.

About Plan_B_Plate

Plan_B_Plate was inspired by the everyday struggle of finding meal ideas with limited ingredients. Whether you're a student or a professional, we often find ourselves asking, "What can I make with what I have?" This project offers a solution by making it easy to discover recipes based on available ingredients. As part of my journey at Holberton School, this project also reflects my passion for technology and cooking. I hope it helps others as much as it has helped me.
